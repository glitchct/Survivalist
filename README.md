# Survivalist

**Hello, Survivalist.**
                            
A **zombie virus** broke out all over the World. Your goal is to **find a cure**.                     
Go with your friends, but when you are infected, you don't have long until **you will become a zombie**.                                
But not only the zombie your enemy. Your "Friends" could become **your enemy**. Watch out and don't get **bitten**.                               
**Try to survive, whether you are with friends or not.**
                             
Good luck. - REDACTED
